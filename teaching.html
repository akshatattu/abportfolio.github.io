Teaching

The Teaching Statement demonstrates my philosophy of composition instruction and describes the methods I devised for integrating current theory and practice in classroom instruction. Initially, my philosophy was confined to teaching literature, but as my teaching experience grew I was able to expatiate on my theory of teaching composition. The statement also describes the activities I devised for teaching writing to my students.
Read analysis here.

I have diligently designed my current English Composition II Syllabus to incorporate the themes of environment, marketing, and media studies. In the classroom, we rhetorically and critically analyze various articles from popular sources such as The Atlantic, The New Yorker, The Telegraph etc. that describe how marketing and media affect the environment.
