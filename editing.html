Editing

Auburn Speaks Edited Manuscript

As a major project of an editing class during my masters, each student edited articles from the magazine Auburn Speaks. The editing involved sentence correction for grammar, clarity, and readability; checking consistency of numbers, fonts, typefaces, abbreviations; positioning of figures and tables etc. My engineering background proved helpful in understanding and improving the technical matter, which can be seen in the sample I present.

International Journal of Acoustics and Vibration Edited Manuscript

The International Journal of Acoustics and Vibration (IJAV) is the refereed journal of the International Institute of Acoustics and Vibration (IIAV). I worked for this journal as an Editorial Assistant during my time at Auburn, and I thoroughly enjoyed editing mechanical engineering research papers. I have edited 60 papers for four issues, and the sample presented here is one of those papers.
